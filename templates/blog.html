Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App

In the era of rapid technological advancements, healthcare accessibility has become essential. We built a symptom-based disease diagnosis web app powered by Flask and Machine Learning to help users identify possible diseases based on their symptoms.

The Problem

People often experience symptoms but lack clarity about what they might mean. Since many diseases share similar symptoms, it‚Äôs difficult for users to identify their health conditions quickly. Our idea was to create an intelligent system that gives instant, data-backed insights.

The Solution

We developed a platform that allows users to enter their symptoms and get disease predictions instantly using an AI model trained on healthcare datasets.

  1. Symptom Input: Users can enter symptoms through a sleek, minimal input form.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier trained on medical data predicts the disease.
  3. Prediction: The system returns accurate possible diagnoses instantly.
  4. Extra Insights: The app suggests precautions, medications, diet, and workout tips.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Modern UI for seamless interaction.
  • Accurate Predictions: Reliable machine learning-based results.
  • Comprehensive Health Info: Includes medication, diet, and precautions.
  • Educational Blogs: Provides additional learning content about diseases and health.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App represents the fusion of AI and healthcare. It empowers users to make informed decisions about their health.
